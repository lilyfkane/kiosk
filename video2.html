  • Good Evening


“Good Evening” transitions into a more somber tone, reflecting life’s darker, quieter moments. Filmed on a gloomy day, it conveys a sense of melancholy and introspection. Yet, woven into this gloom are snippets of Desiderata, a poem I hold close to me. It is an inspiring reminder to persevere and find peace amid life’s sad moments. The Poem’s hopeful words, combined with the dreary visuals, remind us that beauty endures even in times of despair. This video finds light in life’s shadows.
